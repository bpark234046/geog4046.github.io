Book Assignment 04

Swipe map comparison in ArcGIS Online

Partially following Assignment 4 in Chapter 4 of GTKWebGIS, create a new “Story Map Swipe and Spyglass” app from the templates on https://storymaps.arcgis.com, allowing users to show and hide a layer by either moving a divider (swipe map, [example]) or circle window (spyglass map, [example]) across the map.

Process

Do not directly follow the assignment in Chapter 4. Only make a map and app using the Story Map Swipe and Spyglass template.

  1. Find or create a data layer (imagery or features) that would be interesting to compare to a basemap, or two layers to compare to each other.
  2. Create a map and web app on https://storymaps.arcgis.com, showing your layers
  3. Customize, brand, share, and test the app
  4. Add a link, screenshot, and description of the app in your portfolio

Graded Requirements

  1. An ArcGIS Online web app created with the Story Map Swipe and Spyglass template.
  2. Either two layers that make sense to compare to each other, or one layer that would be interesting to compare to a basemap
  3. Customizations to give your app a professional look and feel
  4. A screenshot, description, and link to your web app on your portfolio website

Submit on Moodle

  1. The URL of your web app
