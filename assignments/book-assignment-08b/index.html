Book Assignment 08b

Mobile GIS with ArcGIS Online

Complete Assignment 8B in Chapter 8 of GTKWebGIS, using the chapter tutorial and Q&A as guidance. Create a native app with AppStudio for ArcGIS to collect data and view it in the Operations Dashboard.

Process

Review the tutorial and assignment in the book for a detailed explanation of the process. Below is an overview of the steps involved.

  1. Go to https://appstudio.arcgis.com to create an app using a template other than Quick Report.
  2. Customize and brand the app.
  3. Add an existing feature service or create a new one in ArcGIS Online and add it.
  4. Generate a native app or view it in AppStudio Player for ArcGIS, either on your device or the department tablet.
  5. Take screenshots of the app collecting data and displaying data.

Graded Requirements

  1. An app created in AppStudio and viewed in AppStudio Player or as a generated app for Android or Windows.
  2. Customizations to give your app a professional look and feel
  3. Use of an editable feature service
  4. Two screenshots: the app collecting data and the app displaying data on a map
