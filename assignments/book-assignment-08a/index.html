Book Assignment 08a

Mobile GIS with ArcGIS Online

Complete Assignment 8A in Chapter 8 of GTKWebGIS, using the chapter tutorial and Q&A as guidance. Use the ArcGIS Collector app to record data on a web map and view it in the Operations Dashboard.

Process

Review the tutorial and assignment in the book for a detailed explanation of the process. Below is an overview of the steps involved.

  1. Either create your own web map and feature layer or search for the map provided in the book and save a copy.
  2. Download ArcGIS Collector on your personal device or borrow a department tablet to create records in the feature layer, adding some basic attribute data.
  3. Take screenshots of Collector while entering a point, line, and polygon.
  4. View the records in the Operations Dashboard provided by the book.
  5. Take a screenshot of one of your records in the Operations Dashboard.
  6. Add the screenshots and a description of the work to your portfolio.

Graded Requirements

  1. An editable feature layer in a map in an app
  2. Three screenshots of Collector showing that you entered a point, line, and polygon to a feature layer
