Book Assignment 1

A story map in ArcGIS Online

Complete Assignment 1 in Chapter 1 of GTKWebGIS, using the chapter tutorial and Q&A as guidance, to create a story map with your choice of information and media.

Process

Review the tutorial and assignment in the book for a detailed explanation of the process. Below is an overview of the steps involved.

  1. Create an ArcGIS Online account
  2. Find and prepare the data of your choice, from any source
  3. Create the web map
  4. Create the web app from the map
  5. Configure the app
  6. Share the app
  7. Test the app’s accessibility in incognito/private browsing mode
  8. Add a link and description of the app in your portfolio

Graded Requirements

  1. An ArcGIS Online story map displaying at least 3 features (points of interest)
  2. Images, videos, or text related to your map’s features
  3. Customizations to give your app a professional look and feel
  4. A description and link to your web app on your portfolio website
